# Product Guidelines - Kashmiri Heritage

## Visual Identity
- **Aesthetic:** Organic, Premium, Natural.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Walnut Brown (Deep, earthy base)
  - Saffron Orange (Accents, primary buttons)
  - Forest Green (Success states, nature-inspired accents)
  - Cream/Off-white (Backgrounds, clean space)

## Prose & Messaging
- Tone: Professional, welcoming, and authentic.
- Style: Clear and concise. Focus on the heritage and quality of Kashmiri products.
- Formatting: Use semantic Markdown for all documentation.

## User Experience (UX)
- Mobile-first approach for the frontend.
- Interactive feedback for user actions (cart additions, form submissions).
- Clean navigation with a focus on product discovery.
- Minimalistic design to maintain the "premium" feel.
