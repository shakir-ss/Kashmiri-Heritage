# User Journey: The The Hundred Villages Experience

This document outlines the end-to-end user experience for customers on the Kashmiri General Store platform.

## 1. Discovery & Immersive Browsing
*   **Hero Arrival:** Users land on an immersive hero section featuring high-resolution Kashmiri landscapes and the "Special Harvest" banner.
*   **Visual Storytelling:** Browsing the catalog reveals "Artisan Stories," where each product is presented not just as an item, but as a piece of heritage.
*   **Deep Research:** On the Product Detail page, users explore the "Artisan Heritage" section—learning about the specific hands and regional roots behind their acquisition.

## 2. Personalized Selection
*   **Variant Choices:** Users can customize their order via the interactive Variant Selector (e.g., choosing Saffron weight or Pashmina size).
*   **Dynamic Feedback:** Prices and stock levels update in real-time as variants are selected, ensuring transparency.
*   **Wishlist:** Users can "save for later" using the authenticated wishlist, maintaining a collection of favorites across sessions.

## 3. Trusted Checkout (The Acquisition)
*   **Premium Cart:** A persistent cart allows users to review items with high-quality thumbnails.
*   **Consolidated Checkout:** Users enter their destination. The system automatically calculates "Logistics & Handling" based on a verified Pincode list (providing complimentary shipping for local patrons).
*   **The Commitment Model (Partial COD):** 
    *   To build trust, users can opt for "Partial COD."
    *   They pay a **30% Commitment Fee** upfront to secure artisanal handling.
    *   The **70% Balance** is paid only upon successful delivery.
*   **Security:** Trust badges (SSL, Priority Shipping, Hand-Inspected) are visible throughout the final steps.

## 4. Post-Purchase Engagement
*   **Order Securing:** Upon success, users receive a professional "Transaction Reference."
*   **Real-time Notifications:** An automated WhatsApp confirmation is triggered via Twilio.
*   **Legacy Tracking:** Users can view their full "Order History" to track their acquisition's progress.
